Barbecue Bagel

Ingredients:

  • Bagel of your choosing.
  • Shredded Pork.
  • Any one of Bagel's signature sauces!

Directions:

  1. Toast your favorite Bagel.
  2. Cook Pork to your liking.
  3. Spread your favorite Bagel's sauce on the bagel.
  4. Add Shredded Pork.
  5. Enjoy!
